Pick-A-Book for Friends

When you don’t know what to read next, let a deck of cards decide.

What is this?

This is a zero-backend decision tool for readers with large TBR lists. Upload your Goodreads “to-read” CSV, draw one book per category, and confirm only what you actually plan to read.

Key Rules

  • One draw per category per day
  • Viewing ≠ choosing (only Confirm counts)
  • Confirmed books enter cooldown (default 30 days)

How it works

  1. Export your Goodreads library as CSV
  2. Upload it on this page
  3. Draw candidates by category
  4. Confirm the one you’ll read

All data stays on your device. No login. No tracking.
